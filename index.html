Central Medic Center

Central Medic Center (CMC), a Hospital Management System, is a central modular concept to provide all medical management tools related to Doctor, Patient, Staff etc. CMC is more of an audit trail of doctors encounters with patients. CMC can be a modern way of a hospital treating the patients. CMC covers past history, prescription suggested by doctors, laboratory reports. This means all vital information is residing in the system, which can be readily retrieved to help in making a timely decision.
